Summary: Latitude vs. Max Temperature

The data shows that weather tends to get significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 degrees latitude). Note that the data was collected in January, and that may explain why the weather in the southern hemisphere skews warmer than that in the northern hemisphere as this is a time of year when it is 'winter' in the Northern Hemisphere and 'summer' in the Southern Hemisphere.
