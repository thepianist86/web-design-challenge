Summary: Latitude vs. Humidity

Similar to our data on Cloudiness, overall humidity seems fairly evenly distributed across the X-axis which seems to indicate that humidity is not strongly related to Latitude according to this data. There does seem to be a noticeable concentration of data points with high humidity levels at the time the data was collected. Further analysis might explore if the time of year data is collected affects how humid various parts of the world are.
